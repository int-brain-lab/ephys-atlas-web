Suggested quick-start text

Start by choosing a bucket and a feature in the left column. Then adjust the mapping, statistic, and colormap in the control panel. Use the bar plot and brain views together: hovering and selection are shared across the atlas, so you can inspect a region in multiple representations at once.

The Stats button above the histogram opens the statistical toolbox, which is best used after selecting a small number of regions.
